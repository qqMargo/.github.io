About

I am a final-year PhD candidate in the College of Transportation Engineering at Chang’an University, supervised by Prof. Dawei Hu. My research focuses on life-cycle carbon emission measurement of vehicles, carbon accounting for road transport systems, and policy analysis related to fuel vehicle restriction and transport decarbonisation.
