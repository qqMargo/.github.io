About

I am a doctoral researcher in Transport Planning and Management at Chang’an University, supervised by Prof. Dawei Hu. My work focuses on measuring and analysing carbon emissions across the full life cycle of vehicles, with a particular interest in fuel vehicle restriction policies, urban transport carbon accounting, and data-driven modelling approaches.
